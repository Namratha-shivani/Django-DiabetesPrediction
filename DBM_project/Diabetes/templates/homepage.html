General Information

This website provides information about diabetes.

Diabetes is a chronic condition that affects millions of people worldwide.

It occurs when the body is unable to produce or use insulin properly.

This can lead to high blood sugar levels and other health complications.

Take the test and find out if you are diabetic or prone to diabetes.
